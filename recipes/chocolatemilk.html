Chocolate Milk

Description

Chocolate milk is not just for children, and it is a favorite among adults and athletes alike. This amazing recipe is so easy to make and can be made anywhere.

This recipe is also perfect for the person on the go! Feel free to make this for competitions, late night snacks and even when you just need a nice post gym pick me up!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 tablespoons of Hershey's chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. Pour milk into a glass.
  2. Add Hershey's chocolate syrup and mix well.
