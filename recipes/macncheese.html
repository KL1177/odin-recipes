Macaroni & Cheese

Description

This delicious baked macaroni and cheese is super easy to make, and it is incredibly delicious. A great option for easy weekday dinners. It's also a great way to warm up for the winter nights.

If you want to elevate the dish one step further you can add toppings of your own choice. Some great options include sour cream, chives, green onions, breadcrumbs or even pair it with garlic bread! You cannot go wrong with mixing it up with whatever you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup macaronin shells
  • 1 packet Kraft EZ mac cheese powder
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps

  1. Bring 1 quart of water to a boil.
  2. Add macaroni shells to boiling water and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  3. Drain water from macaroni. Stir in butter and cheese packet.
  4. Let cool 1 minute then enjoy. Add additional toppings of choice if desired.
