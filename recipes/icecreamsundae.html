Ice Cream Sundae

Description

Ice cream sundaes are always a hit at any party, gathering or mealtime. This amazing sundae is incredibly easy to make and sure to satisfy that sugar craving you have. I guarantee this is so simple that even a 5 year old can make this!

Feel free to bust this out at your next party and be a hit among the kids and adults together! Just remember that this will be sure to put anyone in a food coma not too long after!

Ingredients

  • Ice cream flavor(s) of your choosing (we recommend vanilla as a good base)
  • Hot fudge or chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Sprinkles of your choosing (can't go wrong with rainbow sprinkles)

Steps

  1. Scoop your desired amounts/types of ice cream into a bowl.
  2. Add some whipped cream on top of your ice cream in a conical spiral pattern.
  3. Drizzle your hot fudge or chocolate syrup on top of the whipped cream and ice cream.
  4. Add desired amount of sprinkles on top of sundae.
